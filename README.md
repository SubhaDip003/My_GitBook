# HOME

Hello, I’m **Subhadip Sardar** — a cybersecurity professional with 4+ years of experience in this field. My journey blends hands-on expertise in **Networking, Windows/Linux Administration, Ethical Hacking, VAPT, and Cybersecurity** , along with a drive to solve complex problems in the digital world.

This GitBook is my personal knowledge hub where I share:

* **Cybersecurity research & insights**
* **CTF write-ups** to help fellow enthusiasts learn practical skills
* **Technical notes & tutorials** for anyone passionate about security

I believe knowledge grows best when shared. Through this space, my goal is to support learners, professionals, and organizations in strengthening their defenses against ever-evolving cyber threats.

**Welcome aboard — let’s learn, break, and secure together. 🚀**
